Why Mountains Matter

Mountains create memorable experiences because they demand attention. Weather changes quickly, views open up with elevation, and even short hikes can feel like a reset from everyday routine.

  1. Reasons I enjoy mountain environments
    • Big visual contrast between sky, rock, trees, and snow
    • Built-in challenge through distance, elevation, and planning
    • Quiet settings that make it easier to think and recharge
  2. They pair well with photography, trail running, and weekend travel
  3. They make data stories about weather and geography more interesting
